How it works

  1. Upload the PDF that has handwritten annotations or typed review comments.
  2. Optionally upload the original document (.docx) to use as the base for the output.
  3. Choose an OCR engine – Tesseract is free and works locally; OpenAI and Google Cloud Vision give better results for difficult handwriting.
  4. Review the extracted text blocks side-by-side with the PDF pages, add or edit annotations, and choose each annotation type.
  5. Click "Make File" to download a Word document (.docx) with all annotations as Word comments and tracked changes.
OCR Parse — runs locally in your browser • No data is sent to external servers (unless you use a paid OCR engine)
