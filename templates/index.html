How it works

  1. Upload the PDF that has handwritten annotations or typed review comments.
  2. Optionally upload the original document (.docx) to use as the base for the output.
  3. Review the extracted text blocks side-by-side with the PDF pages, add or edit annotations, and choose each annotation type.
  4. Click "Make File" to download a Word document (.docx) with all annotations as Word comments and tracked changes.

🔒 Privacy: Uses free Tesseract OCR – all processing happens on the server. Documents are stored for up to 24 hours, then automatically deleted. You can also delete your document at any time from the review page.
